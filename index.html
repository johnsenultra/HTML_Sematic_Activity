About

  • Creation and Overview: The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is a media franchise and shared universe centered around a series of superhero films and television series produced by Marvel Studios.
  • Inception: The MCU officially began with the release of "Iron Man" in 2008, which was followed by "The Incredible Hulk" later the same year. These films laid the foundation for a connected universe where characters and storylines would cross over and interconnect.
  • Characters: The MCU features a vast ensemble of iconic characters, both heroes and villains.
